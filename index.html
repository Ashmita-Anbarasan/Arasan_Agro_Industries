Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know | About Our Rice

How aged is your rice? +

Our rice is aged anywhere from 3 to 24 months depending on the variety and intended cooking profile. This controlled aging process helps improve aroma, texture, and cooking performance for both daily meals and special occasions.

Where do you source your paddy? +

We source premium paddy varieties from Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and Maharashtra. We work with trusted farmers and suppliers across these regions to maintain consistent grain quality throughout the year.

What's the difference between varieties? +

HMT Sona Ponni: Best for daily meals | Soft & aromatic. Jeera Rice: Perfect for biryanis | Absorbs flavors well. RNR: Economic | Great value for daily use. Each variety has unique characteristics suited for different dishes.

How should I store the rice? +

Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. Avoid direct sunlight. Properly stored rice stays fresh for 6-12 months. Keep away from moisture and strong odors.

Do you offer bulk orders? +

Yes! We supply to restaurants, wedding caterers, and bulk buyers. Special pricing available for large orders. Contact us for quotes and delivery options.

What are your delivery areas? +

We deliver across Tamil Nadu and neighboring states. Contact us for specific delivery timelines and shipping charges to your location.
